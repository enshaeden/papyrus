Users can request access to <BUSINESS_APPLICATION_A> through the **<IDENTITY_PROVIDER> Access Request Form** :

1. Log into **<IDENTITY_PROVIDER>** .
2. Select Request Access
3. Look for **<BUSINESS_APPLICATION_A>** and fill in the requested information
4. Submit an **Access Request** form.

**Approval Rules**

- **Approval Required:** Non-Sales users, contractors, or cross-functional team
- **No Approval Required:**
