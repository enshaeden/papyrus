## Summary

This article outlines the key onsite support information for `<OFFICE_SITE_C>`, including office access, network use, printing, event spaces, and support escalation.

## Office Logistics

- Use the internal office directory for the current address, parking, and reception instructions.
- Keycards and temporary access should be coordinated through the workplace team.
- Report lost keycards or after-hours access failures through the approved support channel.

## Network And Printing

- Use the managed-device wireless network on company-owned endpoints only.
- Use the guest network for personal devices and visitors.
- Follow the office printing guide before adding printers or troubleshooting print failures.

## Event Spaces

- Review the event-request workflow before scheduling large meetings or events.
- Use the conference-room directory for current room capacity and equipment details.
- Escalate room-equipment failures through the AV or service-desk workflow as appropriate.

## Support

- Submit a ticket for onsite issues that cannot be resolved from the local guides.
