# Summary
This article outlines the key logistical and IT support information for employees working from or visiting the <OFFICE_SITE_A> office.

## Office Details
- **Office Address:** <OFFICE_ADDRESS>
- **Office Hours:** Access-card holders can access the site during the approved office hours.
- **Access Cards:** Request access through the office operations workflow or the designated onsite support contact.

## Wi-Fi Access
### Managed network
- Use the managed office Wi-Fi on company devices only.
- Open an IT ticket if the device cannot connect.

### Guest network
- Use the guest network for personal devices or visitors.
- Guest access does not provide access to internal resources.

## Desk Booking
- Use the approved desk-booking process for this office.
- Standard desks provide monitor, dock, and power access.
- Request extra adapters or accessories through the support workflow if needed.
