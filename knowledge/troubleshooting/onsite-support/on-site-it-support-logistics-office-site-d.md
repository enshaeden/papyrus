# Summary
This article outlines the key logistical and IT support information for employees working from or visiting the <OFFICE_SITE_D> office.

## Office Details
- **Office Address:** <OFFICE_ADDRESS>
- **Office Hours:** Permanent badge holders follow the approved access policy. Guest access follows staffed reception hours.
- **Reception Contact:** <EMAIL_ADDRESS>
- **Access Cards:** Request badge support through the office operations workflow.

## Wi-Fi Access
- Use the office Wi-Fi provided at the site.
- Credentials are assigned per user.
- Raise an IT ticket if access fails or if a certificate prompt blocks setup.

## Desk Booking
- Use the approved desk-booking system through the <IDENTITY_PROVIDER> portal.
- Choose an available desk for the required date and cancel unused reservations.
- Standard desks provide a monitor and power.

## Booth and Meeting Space Booking
- Use the published room-resource calendar for booths and meeting rooms.
- Reserve the required resource from the collaboration calendar before arrival.
- Escalate missing accessories or room-equipment issues through IT support.

## Printing
- Connect to the office Wi-Fi before printing.
- Install the approved office printer driver through self-service if required.
- Add the default office printer from system printer settings.
- Confirm the paper size is set correctly before printing.
