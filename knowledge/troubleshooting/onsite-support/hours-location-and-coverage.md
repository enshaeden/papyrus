IT Support covers a 21x5 model due to our geographic distribution. Centered around our <OFFICE_SITE_C> primary office operation, and, therefor, PST, the hours and coverage are as follows:

# Remote-first support

**Sunday** 20:00 PST to **Monday** 17:00 PST (Monday 0830 IST to Tuesday 0530 IST)

**Monday** 20:00 PST to **Tuesday** 17:00PST

**Tuesday** 20:00 PST to **Wednesday** 17:00 PST

**Wednesday** 20:00 PST to **Thursday** 17:00 PST

**Thursday** 20:00 PST to **Friday** 17:00 PST (Friday 0830 IST to Saturday 0530 IST)

# Onsite support (local standard time)

## <OFFICE_SITE_A> (HYD)

| **Monday** | **Tuesday** | **Wednesday** | **Thursday** | **Friday** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 09:00 - 16:30 | 09:30 - 16:30 | 09:30 - 16:30 | Exception only | Exception only |

## <OFFICE_SITE_C> (<OFFICE_SITE_C>)

| **Monday** | **Tuesday** | **Wednesday** | **Thursday** | **Friday** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 08:00 - 17:00 | 08:00 - 16:00 | 08:00 - 17:00 | 08:00 - 16:00 | Exception only |

The goal is coverage from 08:00 to 17:00, expecting two people working three days each over four total days to cover the span. On any day where there is only one person covering, the expectation is coverage from 08:00 to 16:00.

| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 09:00 - 17:00 | 09:00 - 17:00 | 09:00 - 17:00 | Exception only | Exception only |

## <REGION_D> (LON)

**Remote support Monday - Friday** , 09:00 to 17:00

Onsite coverage available on-demand, with advance notification.
