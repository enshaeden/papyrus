## Summary

This article outlines the key onsite support information for `<OFFICE_SITE_B>`, including office access, network use, desk equipment, printing, and storage access.

## Office Logistics

- Use the internal office directory for the current address and access instructions.
- Request access cards through the designated workplace contact.
- Escalate failed badge access or after-hours entry issues to workplace operations.

## Network And Workspace

- Use the managed-device wireless network on company-owned endpoints only.
- Use the guest network for personal devices and visitors.
- Standard desks provide monitor, dock, and power access. Bring any adapter required for your device.

## Printing And Storage

- Follow the office-specific printer setup guide before printing.
- Use the onsite storage cupboard only through the designated office contact.
- Record any loaner or replacement hardware issued from office storage in the asset workflow.

## Support

- Submit a ticket for printer, desk, or onsite support issues that cannot be resolved locally.
