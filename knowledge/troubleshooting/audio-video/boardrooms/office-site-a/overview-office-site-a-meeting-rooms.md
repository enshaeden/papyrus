# Introduction

This document provides general instructions for using meeting rooms at **<COMPANY_NAME> <OFFICE_SITE_A> Hub.** Each room may have different features or equipment. For detailed, room-specific instructions, please refer to the corresponding link listed below.

# Booking a Meeting Room

All meeting rooms should be reserved through <COLLABORATION_PLATFORM> Calendar. **Steps:**

1. Open <COLLABORATION_PLATFORM> Calendar and create a new event.
2. Add your <VIDEO_CONFERENCING_PLATFORM> or messaging add-on meeting link, if needed.
3. Use the "Add rooms" option to select the room.
4. Save the event — the room will be booked automatically.

**Important:** If your meeting is recurring and you won’t be in the office for a particular instance, cancel or release the room booking so others may use the space.

# Meeting Room Best Practices

- Reserve the room in advance and cancel if not needed.
- Arrive early to set up and test equipment.
- Mute your mic when not speaking.
- Leave the room clean, organized and remove personal items.
- Do not unplug or move equipment.
- End your meeting on time for the next group.
