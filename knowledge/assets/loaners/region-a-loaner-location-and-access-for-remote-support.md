# **Repair and emergency loaners**

Loaner laptops will be made available by **Office Physical Security** and stored in the Inventory storage room on 7th Floor of the <OFFICE_SITE_A> office.

# **Process to Get the Loaner Laptop**

1. **IT Team Confirmation**
  1. The IT Support Team must first confirm the hardware issue (e.g., physical damage).
2. **Physical Security Notification**
  1. Upon confirmation, the IT Team will inform the Physical Security personnel on duty to release a loaner laptop.
3. **Laptop Provision:**
  1. Upon the user’s arrival at the Security Desk in HYD, the Security team member on-duty will provide the employee with the IT-designated loaner laptop.

> INFO: **Onboarding Assistance**
>
> The IT Team will assist the employee with the necessary steps to onboard the loaner laptop for immediate work use.
