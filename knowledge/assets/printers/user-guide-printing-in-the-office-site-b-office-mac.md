### **Important first steps:**

1. **For MAC ,** Ensure that you are connected to the **“office Wi-Fi network”** You can also use the Use the Ethernet Cable next to the printer with Windows label on it
2. Open the **Self Service** app on your laptop
3. Install the tile named **primary office Printers**
4. This will add all <OFFICE_SITE_C> and <OFFICE_SITE_B> printers. However, Look for **VAN Loonie** to print in the <OFFICE_SITE_B> office.

**Additional questions or concerns, please create a ticket with** <EMAIL_ADDRESS> **.**

**PRO-TIP - Disconnect VPN while you are printing a document.**
