# Governance & Decisions

This area records the structural rules and durable rationale behind the repository. Use it when you need to understand why the knowledge system is shaped the way it is, what constraints govern changes, and where a repository-level decision was recorded.

## Use The Right Area

- For operator-facing procedures and support execution guidance, use the [Knowledge Base](../knowledge/home/index.md).
- For repository architecture, schema, generator, taxonomy, and workflow design documentation, use [System & Design Docs](../docs/index.md).
- For accepted structural choices with rationale and tradeoffs, use `decisions/`.
