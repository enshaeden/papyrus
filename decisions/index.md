# Governance & Decisions

This area records the structural rules and durable rationale behind the Papyrus control plane. Use it when you need to understand why the repository is shaped the way it is, what constraints govern changes, and where a repository-level decision was recorded.

## Use The Right Area

- For canonical operational knowledge, use `knowledge/` and `archive/knowledge/`.
- For architecture, workflow, and governance design documentation, use `docs/`.
- For accepted structural choices with rationale and tradeoffs, use `decisions/`.
